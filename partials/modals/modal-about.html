What is Divvy

Divvy is an expense splitting application. Perfect for situations when you want to quickly split a collection of expenses between a group of people.

Choose to split evenly or assign expenses at an individual level! Divvy gives you full control over how you want to divvy up those expenses.

Save your results (divvies) locally on to your browser or login with a Google account to save your divvies across different browsers and devices.

If you encounter any bugs or would like to suggest new features, feel free to contact me. I hope you enjoy using Divvy!
